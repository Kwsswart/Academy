Index:

Auth: Auth section assumes responsibility of login, logout and user registration and setting Permission
    auth/forms verifies emails and phone numbers it is essential that you enter country code when entering.
    Permissions always needs a single master without any master you may lose certain items.
    management can place management and below, in their own Academy.
    senior management can place in all.

staff: Staff section deals with editing, removing, avatars
    Avatars are stored: need to find where to store in container

Email: Look into making the application more secure to allow to send emails without errors
            Ensure webmail stmp is setup that way 
    in .env file add:

        MAIL_SERVER=smtp.mail.com
        MAIL_PORT=587
        MAIL_USE_TLS=1
        MAIL_USERNAME=<gmail username>
        MAIL_PASSWORD=<gmail password>
        ADMINS=['user@example.com']
